# English for Construction Course

##  Overview
This repository contains materials from the **English for Construction course** that I designed and delivered.  
The course was created for professionals in the construction industry, focusing on **technical vocabulary, email communication, and practical scenarios**.  

I was able to teach this course thanks to my **knowledge of construction procedures and technical drawings**, which I gained during my studies at a **Technical Secondary School in Environmental Protection**. This background allowed me to connect language learning with real-world engineering contexts.

---

##  Course Objectives
- Introduce learners to **professional construction vocabulary**.  
- Develop skills in **formal and informal email communication**.  
- Strengthen listening, speaking, reading, and writing in **construction-related contexts**.  
- Prepare learners to communicate effectively with suppliers, clients, and international partners.

##  Teaching Methods
- **Vocabulary drills** (flashcards, matching exercises).  
- **Role-play simulations** (supplier communication, site meetings).  
- **Case studies** (defective materials, project delays).  
- **Writing tasks** (formal emails, reports, summaries).  
- **Listening and speaking practice** (construction site scenarios, negotiations).  

---

##  Outcomes
- Learners gained confidence in **using construction-specific English vocabulary**.  
- Developed ability to **write professional emails** (requests, complaints, negotiations).  
- Improved fluency in **technical communication** with suppliers and clients.  
- Prepared for **career development and international collaboration** in the construction sector.
